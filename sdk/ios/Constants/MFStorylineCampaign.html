MFStorylineCampaign

MFStorylineCampaign enum specifies different type of storyline campaign.

  • MFStorylineCampaignText: Storyline for showing alert/notification by text campaign.
  • MFStorylineCampaignImage: Storyline for showing image campaign.
  • MFStorylineCampaignVideo: Storyline for showing video campaign.
  • MFStorylineCampaignUrl: Storyline for showing url webpage campaign.
